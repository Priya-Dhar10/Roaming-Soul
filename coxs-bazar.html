Cox's Bazar

Cox’s Bazar is the world’s longest natural sea beach, stretching over 120 kilometers along the southeastern coast of Bangladesh. Known for its stunning golden sands, vibrant fishing villages, and scenic hill views, it’s a top destination for beach lovers, surfers, and nature enthusiasts. The area also offers rich cultural experiences and delicious seafood, making it a must-visit spot for travelers.
